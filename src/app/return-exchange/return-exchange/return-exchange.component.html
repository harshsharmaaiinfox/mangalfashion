Return & Exchange Policy

Shop with confidence - Easy returns and exchanges

If, for any reason, you are not completely satisfied with a purchase, we invite you to review our policy on returns.

Order Cancellation Rights

You are entitled to cancel your order within 0-7 days without providing any reason for doing so. The deadline for cancelling an order is 0-7 days from the date on which you received the goods.

Conditions for Returns

In order for the goods to be eligible for a return, please ensure that:

  • The goods were purchased within the last 0-7 days.
  • The goods are in the original packaging.

Goods That Cannot Be Returned

  • Goods made to your specifications or clearly personalized.
  • Goods that are not suitable for return due to health protection or hygiene reasons and were unsealed after delivery.
  • Goods that are, after delivery, inseparably mixed with other items.
